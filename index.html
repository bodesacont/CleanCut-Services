Questions

Frequently Asked

How much does it cost?
Lawn mowing starts at $25–$50 depending on yard size. Car detailing starts at $30–$60. Book a free consultation and I'll give you an exact quote with no pressure.
What area do you serve?
Currently serving neighborhoods in and around Indianapolis. If you're not sure, just book a consultation and ask — I'll let you know if I can make it work.
Do you bring all the supplies?
Yes! I bring my own mower, trimmer, edger, and all car cleaning supplies. You don't need to provide anything except access to your yard or vehicle.
What if it rains?
I'll reach out the day before to confirm. If weather makes a job impossible, we'll reschedule for the next available slot — no charge.
How do I pay?
Cash, Venmo, or CashApp — all accepted. Payment is due after the job is done and you're happy with the results.
Can I set up a recurring schedule?
Absolutely! Weekly and bi-weekly lawn mowing plans are available. Just mention it when you book and we'll set up a standing schedule.
